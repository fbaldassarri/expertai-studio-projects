ASUS Zenbook-13 PC - rated with 2 stars
Conan O'Connor of Springfield, CO
Original review: Aug. 09, 2011
I am not impressed by this laptop. It looks sharp but it's not so good. Screen is bright but the battery doesn't last very long. It doesn't seem to be very durable nor reliable. It's an ok computer for the price but I wish I bought something else for little more money.
